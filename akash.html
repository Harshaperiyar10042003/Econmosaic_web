Hurdles of an Invisible Automobile Capital.

At today’s electrifying performances and lightning developments of technological and industrial growth at Tamilnadu, especially Hosur an industrial town with its attractive climate also known as ‘Little England’ is also creating a manufacturing revolution alike to the industrial revolution, which took place at britain post first world war.

❖ The emerging automobile capital of tamilnadu with its close proximity to bangalore is seen as a potential investment across many sectors.

❖ Whereas, we will look upon the decisions taken by the state governments of tamilnadu and karnataka, In the past few months after the inauguration of the First Inter State Link project of southern india by PM Narendra Modi on Aug10 2025.

After the announcement of the project, TN govt took steps to fast track the Detailed Feasibility Report (DFR) and even funded for the initiative.

❖ On the late of october21,2025.BMRCL (Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited) submitted a DFR stating that the Namma metro trains use 750 V DC third traction but the CMRL (Chennai Metro Rail Limited) proposed to integrate the power line of 25 kV AC overhead traction, which is not possible and the project with high hopes and expectations has hit a road block.

This Bangalore – Hosur Metro project covers a total span of 23kms which consist IT companies with large IT employees who spend half their productive time at traffic signals and sacrifice their mental well-being for their employment.

In side with this hurdle, another important infrastructure development was the establishment of an international airport at Hosur, to facilitate faster connections between southern districts of tamilnadu and to serve as an investment destination of MNC’s.

This plan was halted due to the AAI ( Airport Authority of India ) regulations where, when the KIA (Kempegowda International Airport) was established. Regulations such as no other airport should not be established within 150km radius until 2033 other than some domestic airports located within karnataka.

Did You Know?

The establishment of international airport will attract more than 30million passengers whereas at present, The Int’l airport located at chennai receives only 22 million commuters annualy.

Due to stalling of these infrastructure projects, the creative mind and productive time of individuals goes to drain by spending their work hours in traffic and their long travel.

While working for a better tommorow state governments should keep their hate politics aside and strive to establish and implement, infrastructure and policies that ensures it’s people a better mobile connectivity to expand their scope of opportunities.

The union government with its silence in these matters creates a vaccum in its accountability nature, which showcases a lack of involvement in the establishment and the functioning of the project

TN govt focuses to help the place achieve its full potential and to attract sustainable investment,to help in the growth of states economy and ensure employment for the unemployed youths in the state.

Barren lands has seen an enormous rise of land value which are near metro station which is wanted by commuters to reduce their travel time to their workplace.
